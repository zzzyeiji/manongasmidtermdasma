Dasmariñas City is a vibrant and rapidly growing city located in the province of Cavite, Philippines. Known for its rich history and cultural heritage, Dasmariñas has transformed from a small municipality into a bustling urban center, making it one of the most populous cities in the country.


The city is strategically located approximately 30 kilometers south of Manila, making it an ideal residential area for those working in the capital. With a land area of 90.1 square kilometers, Dasmariñas is home to a diverse population that reflects the dynamic nature of urban life.


Dasmariñas City is often referred to as the "University Capital of Cavite" due to the presence of several prestigious educational institutions, including De La Salle University – Dasmariñas, National University Dasmarinas, and the University of the Philippines Dasmariñas. These universities contribute significantly to the city's vibrant academic community and provide quality education to thousands of students.


The city is also known for its rich cultural heritage, with various festivals and events celebrated throughout the year. The annual "Pahimis Festival," which showcases the local culture, arts, and cuisine, attracts both locals and tourists alike. The city's commitment to preserving its history and culture is evident in its well-maintained historical sites and landmarks.


As a first-class city, Dasmariñas continues to develop its infrastructure and services to meet the needs of its growing population. The local government is dedicated to providing quality public services, promoting economic growth, and ensuring a sustainable environment for future generations.
